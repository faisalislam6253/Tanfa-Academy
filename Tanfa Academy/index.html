Welcome to Tanfa Academy

Tanfa Academy is an online school where students from classes 6 to 12 can attend classes in both Bangla and English mediums. It offers high-quality education through live and interactive online sessions, ensuring that students can learn from anywhere. For dedicated and ambitious learners, Tanfa Academy provides a special science batch focusing on subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics to help them achieve outstanding results. Along with academic courses, the academy also offers a web development course, giving students the opportunity to gain modern technical skills and build websites from scratch. Tanfa Academy aims to make learning flexible, effective, and future-ready for every student.
